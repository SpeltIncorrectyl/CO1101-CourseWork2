TechCon2023

General

TechCon is an annual conference concering upcoming technological developments from the world's leading companies. This year's theme is Space Exploration. We have talks from engineers from Nasa concerning the Juice (jupiter icy moon explorer) probe and its launch ealier this year. But space is not all. We of course have our usual speakers and pannels: topics ranging from the very recent invention of large language models to developments in solar energy.

Our headline speaker this year is Cleve Ash from inference.ai, among others from Nasa, Abundant Energy, more. You can see a full list of speakers on the speakers page.
